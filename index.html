ABOUT

I am a PhD student studying astronomy at the University of Tokyo. I'm doing my PhD at the National Astronomical Observatory of Japan, Mitaka, Tokyo. My research interest is galaxy formation and evolution. I'm working to explore how galaxies have formed and evolved with observational data obtained by state-of-art telescopes, for example, ALMA, Subaru, HST, and JWST.

My PhD is ongoing with wouderful supervisers, Prof. Seiichi Sakamoto, Dr. Yuichi Matsuda, Dr. Ken-ichi Tadaki, and Dr. Yuichi Harikane.

Sincerely grateful to everyone around me.

RESEARCH

My research interest is galaxy formation and evolution.

I'm investigating when and how galaxies form across the cosmic time with observational data obtained by large telescopes (e.g., ALMA, Subaru, HST, and JWST).
